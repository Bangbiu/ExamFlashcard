A lesion to this structure would cause problems with?

Image: Brain Regions at https://canvas.wisc.edu/courses/279416/quizzes/327587

Explanation:

The primary motor cortex is responsible for signaling production of skilled movement on the contralateral side of the body; therefore, a lesion here (on the left side) would result in problems with movement on the right side.
